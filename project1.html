GlobalSource Ltd. – Procurement & Supply Chain Dashboard

Business Background

GlobalSource Ltd. is a mid-sized company managing diverse suppliers across multiple categories. The company faced challenges like inconsistent supplier performance, delivery delays, and inefficient payment processes.

Business Objectives

  • Enhance operational efficiency
  • Reduce procurement costs
  • Strengthen supplier relationships
  • Enable data-driven decision-making through analytics

Key Challenges

  • Supplier Performance – Measuring reliability & cost-effectiveness
  • Order Efficiency – Minimizing delays and cancellations
  • Category Optimization – Identifying high-demand categories for negotiation
  • Payment Bottlenecks – Improving payment speed and accuracy
