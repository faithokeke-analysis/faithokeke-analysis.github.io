Procurement & Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard

Tool: Power BI | Domain: Procurement & Supplier Management

📌 Business Background

GlobalSource Ltd., a mid-sized company managing diverse suppliers, faced:

  • ❌ Inconsistent supplier performance
  • ⏳ Delays in deliveries
  • 💸 Inefficient payment processes

🎯 Business Objectives

  • Strengthen supplier relationships
  • Enhance operational efficiency
  • Reduce procurement costs
  • Enable data-driven decision-making

⚠️ Key Challenges

  • Supplier performance – reliability & cost-effectiveness
  • Order efficiency – delays & cancellations
  • Category optimization – identifying high-demand categories
  • Payment bottlenecks – slow or inaccurate payments

🚀 Project Goal

Transform procurement data into actionable insights with Power BI by:

  • Tracking Supplier KPIs
  • Monitoring Order cycles & costs
  • Streamlining Payment analysis

🛠 Tools & Skills Used

  • Power BI – Power Query, DAX, interactive dashboards
  • Data Modeling – linking suppliers, categories, orders, and payments
  • Business Analysis – translating business needs into metrics

📊 Dashboard Highlights

  • Supplier Performance: On-time delivery, cost-effectiveness
  • Order Cycle Efficiency: Delays, cancellations, average lead time
  • Payment Analysis: Paid vs. unpaid, average processing time
  • Category Analysis: High-demand items for negotiation

💡 Why This Matters

Procurement inefficiencies = higher costs. Analytics helps:

  • 🤝 Negotiate better contracts
  • 📈 Improve supplier accountability
  • 💰 Ensure smoother cash flow

✅ Outcome

This dashboard provided actionable insights, helping GlobalSource Ltd. optimize suppliers, improve order efficiency, and streamline payments.
